OUR DREAM = MUSICAL EQUALITY

Imagine a world where female guitarists fill the cities with music
alongside their male counterparts. We want to live in that world.

THE REVOLUTION HAS BEGUN

R E V O L Y O U T I O N

Bringing musical equality is not easy, but it's not impossible if we join hands. We have gathered a strong group of people and started the revolution, but we need people like you to keep it growing. Join us on this global journey to identify awesome female guitarists and help us reach our milestones.

Let's make this big dream come true - together!
